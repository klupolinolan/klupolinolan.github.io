ABOUT KAYLA LUPOLI-NOLAN

Kayla Lupoli-Nolan is a senior at Stony Brook University on Long Island, New York with a planned graduation date of December 2019. In her time at Stony Brook, she has learned how to manage classwork with her reporting without having to risk the quality of either. She has also learned multimedia and camera skills that she will carry with her into her career.

In the future, she plans to work in the San Francisco area with a concentration on important issues in the Bay Area.
